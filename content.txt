At Mastermind Productions, we believe it’s important that music aligns with the message you
want to share with your listeners. Creating music is central, but there’s much more involved.
That’s why we also offer services such as sound engineering, distribution, and consultation,
helping projects be completed more ef ciently and helping your music stand out.
We have worked on projects with, among others, Top Notch, Red Bull, LASER by Sony

Productie
Wij werken zowel op afstand als op locatie en leveren producties in diverse genres zoals Pop,

muziek releasen moet voor iedereen toegankelijk zijn.

Consultatie
Zoek je ondersteuning in de vorm van een aanspreekpunt? Heb je iemand nodig die je helpt

Services
Productions
We work remotely and on location, delivering productions in various genres such as Pop, Hip
Hop, Afro, and Dancehall. Together, we discuss what you are looking for and the criteria the
production needs to meet. Everything is fully custom made and tailored to the wishes of the
organization or artist. Our producers are also available for writing camps.

Distrubition
Do you want to release your music online but don’t know where to start? We help with
registration, creating your own artist page, and ensuring your single, EP, or album reaches the
right distribution platforms. No matter the genre, releasing music should be accessible to
everyone.

Sound engineer
We take care of the recording process. This means we record your vocals and process the
recording so you have a product that’s ready to move on to the next stage of production.
Thanks to our experience across different genres, we know how to best record and treat
vocals so your sound can be fully developed. If you’re only looking for a mix or master, you
can also get in touch with us.

Consultatie
Are you looking for support in the form of a point of contact? Do you need someone to help
you with the business side or to guide you in nding what suits you best? So you can fully
focus on the creative process. We’re happy to think along with you about your journey and
your ideas. If you run into anything or feel like something is missing, we’re here to support
you.
